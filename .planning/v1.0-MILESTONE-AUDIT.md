## Executive Summary

Milestone v1.0 delivers a 4-phase system (Environment Substrate → Core Orchestration → Specialist Execution → Monitoring Uplink). All 4 phases have completed plan execution with SUMMARY.md documentation. However, **only Phases 1 and 2 have formal VERIFICATION.md files**. Phases 3 and 4 are "unverified phases" — their work is documented in SUMMARYs but never passed through the verification gate.

**8 of 16 requirements are fully satisfied** (verified by VERIFICATION.md + SUMMARY.md + traceability). The remaining 8 are **partial** — work was completed per SUMMARYs but lacks formal verification.

**4 of 6 E2E flows are wired end-to-end.** Two flows have gaps: L1→L2 delegation (missing L1 config.json) and snapshot capture (directory not initialized).
